Skillvanto Email Signatures

How to add your signature to Gmail:
1. Find your name below
2. Click and drag to select everything inside the dashed box
3. Copy (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)
4. Open Gmail → Settings → See all settings → General → Signature
5. Click inside your signature box → Paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)
6. Click Save Changes
